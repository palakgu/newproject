Beagle

The Beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The Beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare. With a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the Beagle is employed as detection dogs for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. Read More
